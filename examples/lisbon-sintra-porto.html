Road Trip Itinerary

Lisbon, Sintra & Porto

A one-way run up Portugal, from the Tagus to the Douro. Fly into Lisbon, soak up Sintra and the Atlantic edge, then relocate north to Porto and the port cellars of Gaia, no backtrack required.

Sep 10 to 14, 2026 · 4 travelers · one rental SUV
Lisbon (LIS)→ Base 1: Lisbon (Alfama)→ Sintra loop→ Base 2: Porto (Ribeira)→ Porto (OPO)
Two bases, one direction. You sleep two nights in central Lisbon (Thu, Fri), then relocate north on Saturday for two nights in Porto (Sat, Sun). The relocation is the point: flying into LIS and out of OPO means Porto cannot be a day trip. A round trip from Lisbon would cost roughly 6 to 7 hours of driving (about 310 km each way), and your departure airport sits on the wrong end. Moving the base north on Saturday turns that into a single A1 leg of about 3 to 3.5 hours, with room for one mid route stop. Keep the SUV parked in a garage in town; Alfama and Ribeira streets are too narrow and steep to drive.

Two things that cannot slip

  • The one way LIS to OPO automatic SUV, with the drop fee and a Via Verde toll device confirmed. Book today: automatics and one way allocations disappear first, and the whole route depends on the car existing.
  • An early Pena Palace timed slot for Fri Sep 11, paired with a same day Regaleira slot. Book now on parquesdesintra.pt; without it the Sintra, Cabo da Roca and Cascais loop collapses under a roughly 3 hour walk-up queue.

Book before it sells out

  • One way LIS to OPO automatic SUV: about EUR 250 to 450 for the car, plus a one way drop fee of EUR 50 to 150, Via Verde toll device about EUR 2 per day, and the A1 toll about EUR 23. Book 2 to 3 months out, so now.
  • Pena Palace full ticket (park plus interior), early Fri Sep 11: EUR 20 per person on parquesdesintra.pt. Morning slots go weeks ahead.
  • Quinta da Regaleira, Fri Sep 11 afternoon: EUR 15 per person, self guided, on regaleira.byblueticket.pt. Pair same day with Pena.
  • Jeronimos plus Belem Tower timed slots, Thu Sep 10 afternoon: about EUR 18 to 20 combined. The only Belem window, since Monday is closed.
  • Douro Valley small group day tour, Sun Sep 13: EUR 85 to 120 per person, 2 to 4 weeks out. Not Monday.
  • Livraria Lello voucher, about 9:00 slot Sun or Mon: EUR 10 per person, redeemable against a book.
